Short Film Les Aliénés

15 June 2017

Context

Les Aliénés is a short film created by 10 HETIC students in 10 days. From scenario to communication, though the search for actors, housing, organization, filming, editing of the film and making-of, campaign tracking, website, trailer, posters and so on…

What i did

I was stage / accessory manager and pyrotechnician, this was the funniest part of my job. The rocket was really so funny to build and to animate. I was web developer too, and made the website for the communication, put a lot of tracking codes for the events and communication campaigns, and make it SEO friendly.

Additionally, i was cash manager and make an automated Google Sheet to improve and facilitate the calculation of the investments of the team.
