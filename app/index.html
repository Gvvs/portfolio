AMP Fender tweed princetone 5F2A

What is it

According to my passion to play the guitar, and to love vintage sounds, i built entierely a vintage guitar tube amplificator (no this is not about HTML AMP format...). I got on the web the reel 1962's 5F2A layout and schematics writed at the age by Fender.

All the parts are new in box (NIB), which means built nearly in 1960, never used, and with same specifications as possible. Only the metal cage, the transformer, the tweed tolex and the speaker are new or not made by myself.

How i did

I analyzed and studied the components, bought all the parts on about 15 differents place based on USA, Australia, Paris, and Great Britain. I make a new 3D layout to build the massiv wood box. I soldered all the parts on the bulletin board, pasted the tweed tolex on the wood box, and switch ON !

What did it becomes

This tube AMP is still in my living-room with my pre-CBS Fender stratocaster, and it like to caress my ears.

Automated freshwater aquarium

What is it

I wanted to practice C++ language and make a reel project with an Arduino. So i decided to make from scratch a 130 liters freshwater aquarium with his own electric system, an Arduino controller and a touchescreen integrated into the furniture for the user interface.

The Arduino controller was able to manage the internal temperature and the hydrogen potential of the water, the intensity and the wavelenght of the led lighting, the injection of liquid fertilizer, it was able to fedding fish and to automatically renew the water.

More details

Potential hydrogen of the water was controlled by a solenoid valve on a carbon dioxide cylinder, and was measured by a pH probe. The led lighting has been studied by myself to match with true cycle of photosynthesis, and true cycle of intensity and wavelenght of the sun rays, taking into account the attenuation due to the height of water. Injection of liquide fertilizer, and the renewal of water was managed by peristaltic pumps. The fish feeder was mechanical and maked by myself.

All this was configurable without exception with the touchscreen user interface. Even the intensity in lumens and the wavelenght in Kelvin of each LED, minutes by minutes.

What did it becomes

I sold this project cause it was finished, and it's less funny when there is nothing more to do.

Digital strategy for a psychologist

Growing fast

I worked for Margot Duvauchelle, a clinical psychologist in Amiens, who wanted to start his liberal carrier after having worked at the hospital. She wanted to have a digital strategy to have patients quickly and make herself known. She also wanted to know if i could do some workflow optimizations, especially for it's accounting and to prevent cancellations of appointments.

In summary

First of all, i spent a lot of time analyzing his competition, best search queries in it's area, and to find a compromise between the digital marketing and the ethic imposed by its profession and herself. Then i started the development of her website, paying attention to search engine optimizations. During this time, i started the social media and referer strategy, the local SEO, and give her a lot of advice to make usefull web contents.

Then i started the workflow optimizations. I make an automatic accounting software coded in JavaScript, connected with her Google calendar and her Google contacts, let she have usefull statistics, turnover, taxe declaration and predict how much the URSSAF will take from her.

Finally, to prevent cancellations of appointments, i did an other automatic software that sends sms to her patients 2 days before their appointments.

Debriefing

Her website stay at the first or the second search engine results position (on the 4 best queries) and she can no longer take a patient. Cancellations are low compared to the average. She is angry because she spends too much time on the phone.
